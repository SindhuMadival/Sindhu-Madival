Mysore[10] (/maɪˈsɔːr/ (About this soundlisten)), officially Mysuru ([ˈmaɪ'suːɾu] (About this soundlisten)), is a city in the southern part of the state of Karnataka, India.[11] Mysore city is geographically located between 12° 18′ 26″ north latitude and 76° 38′ 59″ east longitude. It is located at an altitude of 770 m (2,530 ft) above mean sea level.
